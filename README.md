# Progress Knight Enhanced

A heavily expanded version of Progress Knight with new progression paths, dark magic mechanics, jobs, upgrades, quality-of-life improvements, and additional content.

## Features

### Dark Magic System
- Unlock powerful dark magic abilities.
- Train forbidden arts to gain unique bonuses.
- Progress through increasingly dangerous dark magic tiers.
- Sacrifice resources for greater power.

### New Jobs
- Additional careers beyond the original game.
- Dark magic-exclusive professions.
- Higher-end progression paths with unique requirements.
- Improved late-game scaling.

### Expanded Progression
- More goals to achieve.
- Additional unlocks and milestones.
- Extended endgame content.
- New ways to develop your character.

### Quality of Life Improvements
- Improved interface.
- Better organization of tabs and progression systems.
- Additional information displays.
- Smoother gameplay experience.

## About

Progress Knight Enhanced is a fan-made expansion that builds upon the original Progress Knight concept while adding new mechanics, progression systems, and content for players who want a longer and deeper experience.

## Main Additions

- Dark Magic progression tree
- New Dark Magic jobs
- Expanded amulet progression
- Additional unlockable content
- Balance adjustments
- UI improvements
