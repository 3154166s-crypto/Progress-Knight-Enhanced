  • You stumble across a strange looking amulet on your 25th birthday. It does not look like it has any worth on the market given that it's made from cheap-looking copper. However, you feel a weird urge to keep the amulet, so you slip it into your pocket for safekeeping.
  • On your 45th birthday, you feel the amulet shiver uncontrollably in your pocket. A strange etching appears on its centre — the outline of an eye.
  • On your 65th birthday, a living eyeball emerges from the centre of the amulet. It stares at you with an unsettling intelligence.
    By touching the eyeball, you will be reborn, losing all levels and coins. You will gain xp multipliers equal to: 1 + max level / 10. Something tells you the amulet might transform further after 2 whole centuries...
  • The eye watches you. Something stirs beneath it... you sense the amulet is not done with you. Perhaps after 2 whole centuries, it will speak.
  • At age 200, the amulet seems to respond to you somehow. You sense you could use it to embrace something darker.
    Embracing evil resets everything — levels, coins, max levels, and your town. But you gain evil and unlock dark magic.
  • You get the sense the amulet has more to offer if you make it to 350 years.
  • At age 350, the amulet offers you a deal.
    The amulet offers a Pact of Dominion. You lose all evil and coin, but your lifespan is permanently extended by 100 years and all job income is doubled forever.
  • Something about the amulet has changed since the pact. It feels closer to you now. You suspect 500 years is when that changes further.
  • At age 500, the amulet's power becomes part of you permanently. You're offered a chance to give up everything for something more lasting.
    Ascending strips everything. Every level, every coin, every rebirth bonus. In return you gain a permanent x? to ALL xp that can never be lost, and unlock the hidden Celestial job track.
  • At age 750, the amulet opens up a darker path.
    Unlock the Dark Dominion. Dark magic jobs gain massive income and evil becomes a source of power rather than corruption.
  • You have a feeling something else is waiting for you at age 750.
  • You have ascended 0 time(s). Each ascension compounds your power further.
    The cycle continues. Each time you ascend, the multiplier grows. How far can you go?
  • At age 750, the amulet leaves you for good. In its place, you're left with something new.
    A new power stirs. Your ascension multiplier doubles and a fragment of the void permanently reduces all your expenses by 25%.
  • You keep thinking about the number 1000. Something tells you that's significant.
  • At age 1000, you reach a permanent state where future rebirths no longer cost you anything.
    The Eternal Convergence. You retain everything across all future rebirths — levels, coins, max levels, town. Nothing can be taken from you again. Your ascension multiplier is tripled permanently.
  • THE DARK RITUAL

    After 750 years, every dark skill mastered, and your evil at its peak, you have one more option available to you.
