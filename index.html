  • You stumble across a strange looking amulet on your 25th birthday. It does not look like it has any worth on the market given that it's made from cheap-looking copper. However, you feel a weird urge to keep the amulet, so you slip it into your pocket for safekeeping.
  • On your 45th birthday, you feel the amulet shiver uncontrollably in your pocket. A strange etching appears on its centre — the outline of an eye.
  • On your 65th birthday, a living eyeball emerges from the centre of the amulet. It stares at you with an unsettling intelligence.
    By touching the eyeball, you will be reborn, losing all levels and coins. You will gain xp multipliers equal to: 1 + max level / 10. Something tells you the amulet might transform further after 2 whole centuries...
  • The eye watches you. Something stirs beneath it... you sense the amulet is not done with you. Perhaps after 2 whole centuries, it will speak.
  • At age 200, a mouth tears open below the eye: "So you've made it this far... interesting. Are you ready to embrace what you truly are?"
    Embracing evil resets everything — levels, coins, max levels, and your town. But you gain evil and unlock dark magic.
  • The mouth whispers again as you sleep: "There is more. Survive to 350 years... and I will offer you something far greater than evil."
  • At age 350, the amulet floats from your pocket on its own. A second eye opens beside the first. Between them, etched in fire: "YOU ARE OLDER THAN KINGDOMS."
    The amulet offers a Pact of Dominion. You lose all evil and coin, but your lifespan is permanently extended by 100 years and all job income is doubled forever.
  • The second eye drifts closed after the pact is sealed. But you feel something new — a faint heartbeat inside your chest, as if the amulet is becoming part of you. 500 years... that is when it will complete the merge.
  • At age 750, black veins spread across the amulet. A third eye opens and shadows spill from it. Ancient entities whisper forgotten names and offer forbidden power.
    Unlock the Dark Dominion. Dark magic jobs gain massive income and evil becomes a source of power rather than corruption.
    • At age 500, the amulet dissolves into your chest. You feel it fused with your ribs. A voice — your own, but older — whispers: "You have outgrown mortality. Only one thing remains."
    Ascending strips everything. Every level, every coin, every rebirth bonus. In return you gain a permanent x? to ALL xp that can never be lost, and unlock the hidden Celestial job track.
  • The heartbeat inside your chest grows louder. Not faster — just deeper, like something ancient waking up. You hear nothing, but feel a word press against the inside of your ribs: 750.
  • You have ascended 0 time(s). Each ascension compounds your power beyond mortal comprehension.
    The cycle continues. Each time you ascend, the multiplier grows. How far can you go?
  • At age 750, the amulet tears free from your chest on its own. It hovers before you, cracked open like an egg. Inside — nothing. Darkness. Then a voice, neither male nor female, neither kind nor cruel: "You were never the wielder. You were always the vessel."
    A new power stirs. Your ascension multiplier doubles and a fragment of the void permanently reduces all your expenses by 25%.
  • The void inside you hums. Not with power — with anticipation. You count the years without meaning to. 1000. That number keeps surfacing. Like something is waiting at the end of it.
  • At age 1000, time stops. Not metaphorically. The world freezes — leaves hang mid-fall, flames hold their shape, people stand like statues. Only you move. The amulet is gone. In its place, a mark burned into your palm: an eye within a circle within a void.
    "A thousand years," your own voice says, from somewhere outside you. "You have become the thing that watches. The thing that was here before the first kingdom and will remain after the last star."
    The Eternal Convergence. You retain everything across all future rebirths — levels, coins, max levels, town. Nothing can be taken from you again. Your ascension multiplier is tripled permanently.
  • ⚫ THE DARK RITUAL ⚫

    The amulet has gone cold. Its three eyes are shut. Black ichor weeps from the cracks in its copper face. After 750 years of unlife, every dark skill mastered, and your evil overflowing — the amulet opens its eyes all at once and speaks for the last time:

    "You are no longer of this world. You have consumed it. Complete the ritual... and become the darkness itself."
    Requirements: Age ≥ 750 · Evil ≥ 25,000 · Eternal Corruptor reached · Lich's Essence ≥ 500 · Void Pact ≥ 300

    REWARD: You win. The game ends. You are the Darkness.
